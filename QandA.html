Question And Answer With The Punks

Q: How do the players feel now that Daryl Matthews has left the team?
A: “It was a tough blow but come on! We’re still six time Super Bowl champions and make way more money than everyone combined in the room” – Chicago Punks Quarterback


Q: What led to all this success in such a short time?
A: “We had a great leader in the locker room and we all saw the light at the end of the tunnel. The end of the tunnel consisted of hot chicks and checks with many zeros” -Chicago Punks Running Back


Q: Your shop zone started selling unique items recently. Why is this?
A: “The Punks realize that the fans always want more. What better way to give more than to sell bibs for your baby or a carpet so every time you walk on it, you feel greatness.” –Charles Grimes


Q: What would you like to say to your fans?
A: “You’re Welcome for providing you with something to brag about to your friends.” –Chicago Punks outside Line Backer


Q: Will Daryl Grimes ever return?
A: “Probably not. He’s too busy making movies now and I heard he’s on his 7th wife. You can say his plate is very full at the moment.” –Charles Grimes


Q: What can we expect in the 2015-2016 season?
A: “Wins, parties, and lots of wings cause we need protein to keep going and being the best ever” -Chicago Punks Quarterback
