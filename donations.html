Build our home

One of Chicago Punks greatest goal is to have our own stadium, and we are very thankful for every donation we have received. Since the creation of our team, we have been hosting our games in the Chicago Soldier Field, but we believe that is time for our own home. Our goal to have our own stadium is every day closer. To make donations please select and amount from the dropdown box. The dropdown box will have options of 100, 500, or 1000 dollars.

Chicago Punks fund

Chicago Punks fund, is a fund use to seek talent in different universities across the country. With this fund we support talented college students that are interested in becoming part of Chicago Punks team. This fund has been a great success, through Chicago Punks history we have had over 45 players that became part of us and that brought great success to our team. To help us recruiting the best players across the country please make donations by selecting and amount from the dropdown box. The dropdown box will have options of 100, 500, or 1000 dollars.

Thank you

All contributions are welcome, whether you decide to help us to build our home, or you decide to help us recruit the best players across the country, we are very thank full for your help. Also remember that by making donations you will qualify to receive discounted price tickets for all games play it in Chicago.
